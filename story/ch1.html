Chapter 1: From Dustbin to King

The pencil's journey took it through various adventures across London and beyond. It met characters like Mr. Darcy (a bookworm) and Jane Austen (who thought he could never be a writer). But the pen always seemed reluctant, as if there was something more pressing on its mind. One day, when the pencil was lost in a bookstore, it found itself at a stationery store with a map of New York City. There, it met an old book dealer named Oliver who had just finished his last sale and needed someone to help him get rid of a collection of forgotten books.
